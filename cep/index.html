Aussies eligible for latest government rebates get health cover from just $2.93 per day

Little known health insurance rebates allow eligible Australians to get hospital & extras cover for under $21 / week

By Audrea B.

In 2021, the Australian Government is rebating up to 32.8% of the cost of your health insurance policy depending on factors like age and income level. We can help you figure out how much of a rebate you’re entitled to.

Health insurance rates just went up. Again. That’s two increases in 6 months, or an average of $230.26 more to pay every year for hard working Aussie families.

But what few people realise is that there are government rebates out there that could save you hundreds of dollars per year on your cover. For eligible Aussies, the starting cost of a combined hospital & extras policy comes in at under $21 / week (or $2.93 per day) once the rebate has been claimed.

The Government will now pay up to 32.8% of your policy depending on your age, income level, marital status, and cover history. If you’re not claiming this rebate you could be leaving hundreds of dollars on the table every year.

Talk to an advisor now and find out if you’re eligible for the rebate and find out how much you could be saving.

No, it’s not a scam. And no, it’s not magic. In fact, it’s incredibly simple. Then we go out and try to find you a policy without any ‘fat’ - just one that meets your requirements.
