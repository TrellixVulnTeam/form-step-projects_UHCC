These Devices Do More Than Just "Aid" Your Hearing

The leading hearing aid manufacturer, Signia, just launched its smallest and most powerful weapons against hearing loss.

In the last couple of years, many of the world’s best hearing aid engineers have been working tirelessly on these top-secret projects to develop new technology that’s so small and sophisticated that it truly changes people’s lives.

The result: In 2021, Signia (formerly Siemens) finally revealed its ultimate secret weapons: the amazing Xperience technology platform including three devices that are smaller, stronger, and smarter than ever before! The devices are the Active Pro X, Styletto X and Silk X — and they will change our life.

Styletto X, Active Pro X and Silk X.

These high-tech hearing aids are "mumble-free"

Signia’s latest Xperience technology platform has made the Active Pro, Silk and Styletto as high-tech as the latest smartphones.

They truly are mini-computers that can pick up voices and reduce background noise automatically.

The Xperience devices use a unique, speech-processing algorithm that is clinically proven to deliver better-than-normal hearing.

It also enhances a person’s voice while automatically reducing background noise. This means you can understand every word clearly, even in noisy environments like restaurants.

So, no more mumbling sounds or asking people to repeat themselves all the time. In fact, people who’ve tested these devices report a sweeping 50% improvement in speech clarity.

The Silk and Styletto are so small they disappear inside and behind your ear; the Active Pro is often mistaken for wireless earbuds.

Smartphone connectivity for maximum control and ultimate discretion

The Active Pro, Silk and Styletto allow you to have full control of your hearing from the palm of your hand.

Adjust the volume, bass, and treble with just a few taps on your mobile phone. Just use the app to create a personalized listening experience for any occasion.

If you’re often on the go: the rechargeable Active Pro and Styletto models feature a portable charging case and Bluetooth connectivity so you can stream your favorite music, TV shows, and more directly into your hearing aids no matter where you are.
